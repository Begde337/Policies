Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22/03/2025

BegdeDEV (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our game, Berat Yollarda, manages any user data.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal or non-personal data from users. The Game is built to operate entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

2. No Third-Party Services

The Game does not utilize third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No user information is shared with third parties.

3. Minimal Permissions

The Game only uses essential device permissions required for its core functionality (for example, saving game progress). No unnecessary permissions are requested.

4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any data, the Game is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly gather any information from children under 13.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any modifications will be indicated by a change in the “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the Game signifies acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our website: begdedev.weebly.com.
